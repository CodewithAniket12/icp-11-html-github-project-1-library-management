Borrow textbooks and reference materials for an entire semester to support your academic journey.

Our Library maintains a book bank mainly of 11th and 12th standard text books to help students belonging to economically and socially weaker section of the society. The Book Bank Service provides long-term lending of textbooks and academic materials to students for an entire semester or academic year. This service ensures that all students, especially those in need, have access to essential study resources.

Who can use the Book Bank?

  • Registered students of the institution

  • Priority for economically disadvantaged students (if applicable)

  • Members (Students) with an active library membership

How It Works

  1. Register for the Service: Visit the library and fill out the application form.

  2. Submit Required Documents: Student ID, proof of enrollment, and any financial aid documents (if needed).

  3. Select Your Books: Choose from the available list of textbooks and materials.

  4. Borrow & Use: Keep the books for the entire semester.

  5. Return on Time: Return the books in good condition at the end of the semester.

Book Bank Policies

  • Loan Duration: Full semester or academic year

  • Borrowing Limit: Up to 4 textbooks per student

  • Renewals: Allowed based on availability

  • Late Returns: Subject to fines if not returned on time

  • Book Condition: Books must be returned without significant damage; lost/damaged books must be compensated
