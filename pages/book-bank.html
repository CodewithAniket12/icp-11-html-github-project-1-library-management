BOOK BANK

Borrow textbooks and reference materials for an entire semester to support your academic journey.

Our Library maintains a book bank mainly of undergraduate level text books to help students belonging to economically and socially weaker section of the society. The Book Bank Service provides long-term lending of textbooks and academic materials to students for an entire semester or academic year. This service ensures that all students, especially those in need, have access to essential study resources.

Who can use the Book Bank?

  • Registered students of the institution

  • Priority for economically disadvantaged students (if applicable)

  • Members with an active library membership
