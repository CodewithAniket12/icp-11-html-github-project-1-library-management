BOOK LENDING SERVICE

Welcome to our Book Lending Service, where library members can borrow books and other materials with ease. Our goal is to provide a seamless borrowing experience, ensuring access to knowledge and entertainment for all our users.

How It Works

  1. Browse our online catalog or visit the library to find the book you want.

  2. Check the book's availability.

  3. Log in to your account and reserve the book online or pick it up at the library.

  4. Borrow the book using your library card at the self-checkout kiosk or the circulation desk.

  5. Return the book by the due date to avoid late fees.
