PHOTOCOPYING


  • Our Library offers photocopyingervice to all its members, walk-in users and others.

  • Only single copy of journal articles and book chapters are provided for private study and research purpose.

  • Photo Copy Charges are as follows:

    • Black & White Photocopy: ₹0.50 for library members & ₹1.00 for others

    • Colour Photocopy: ₹5 for library members & ₹10 for others
